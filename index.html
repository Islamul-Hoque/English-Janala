Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes, most of our lessons and exercises are free. Some premium features may require a subscription.
3. Do I need to create an account?
No, you can access many resources without an account. However, creating one allows you to track your progress and save your work.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
Use our vocabulary builder tools, flashcards, and daily word challenges. Practice regularly to improve your retention.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
Yes, we offer certificates for selected courses upon successful completion and assessment.
